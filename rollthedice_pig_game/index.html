GAME RULES

  1. The game has 2 players playing in rounds.
  2. In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as s/he whishes. Each result get added to current player's round score.
  3. However, when the player rolls a 1 (one), all his round score will be lost and it's the next player's turn.
  4. The current player can choose to 'Hold' to transfer the round score to the global score. After that, it's the next player's turn.
  5. The first player to reach 100 points on global score wins the game.
